Polar Life

Polar life is our recent project concerning with saving the life of polar animals. Every year hundred of animals lost their life and displaced because of melting icebergs in the north pole. We mainly focusing on reduce the emission of CO2 by negotiating with different government bodies, introduce renewable energy project throughout different citizenries and rehabilitates the victimized animals through voluntarism and also by the help of professionals.

Reduce Emission

Holding the level of CO2 emission in different factories.

Renewable Energy

Building solar panels throughout the cities to save energy.

Animal Rehabilitation

Save the life of polar animals and rehabilitate them.

Voluntarism

Join our volunteer teams and be a part of our communities.
